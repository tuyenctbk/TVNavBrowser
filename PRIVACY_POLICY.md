Introduction

Lite Browser (the “App”) is developed and maintained by [Your Company Name] ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

Summary

- We do not require user accounts in the App.
- We use third-party services (Firebase, Google Mobile Ads) that may collect analytics and advertising identifiers.
- The App uses a WebView to load pages; page content and browsing behavior may be stored locally (history, bookmarks) on the device.

Information We Collect

1. Information you provide directly
- Bookmarks and history saved locally on your device are stored by the App. This information is not shared with us unless you explicitly export or send it.

2. Automatically collected information
- Usage analytics (Firebase Analytics): events about app usage, screens, feature usage, and crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics).
- Advertising identifiers: if the App includes ads (Google Mobile Ads / AdMob), Google may collect the Advertising ID (AAID) for ad personalization and measurement.

3. Device and technical information
- Device model, OS version, language settings, and similar device-level information to help debug and improve the App.

Why We Collect Information

- To provide and improve the App’s core functionality (bookmarks, history, web browsing).
- To analyze how the App is used and fix crashes or bugs (Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics).
- To serve ads and measure ad performance when AdMob is enabled.

How We Use and Share Information

- Local data (bookmarks, history, caches) is stored on the device and is not uploaded by default.
- We use Firebase services for analytics, crash reporting, and remote configuration. These services are provided by Google; their data handling is governed by Google’s policies.
- If ads are enabled, ad networks (e.g., Google) may collect or receive information necessary to display and measure ads.
- We do not sell personal data.

Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:
- Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, Remote Config)
- Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

Please review each provider’s privacy policy for details on their data practices.

Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:
- INTERNET — required to load web pages and contact remote services.
- ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — used to detect network connectivity for a better browsing experience.
- BLUETOOTH / BLUETOOTH_CONNECT — optional, used when interacting with Bluetooth input devices (limited to certain Android versions).

Data Retention

- Local browsing history and bookmarks remain on the device until the user clears them.
- Analytics and crash logs retained according to the policies of the third-party services used.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information stored by the App, but no method is completely secure. Please do not store highly sensitive information in the App.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

- You can clear the App’s history, bookmarks, and cache from within the App Settings.
- To opt out of analytics and personalized ads, follow your device and Google account settings for Ads personalization and limit ad tracking.
